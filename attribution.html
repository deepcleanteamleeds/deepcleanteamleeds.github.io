Credits and Attribution

At The Deep Clean Team, we believe in the importance of recognizing and giving credit to the talented individuals and resources that have contributed to the creation and enhancement of our website.

Acknowledgement of CodePen Contributions

Our website features innovative design elements and functional components that are the result of the creative genius of several developers from CodePen.io, a platform that fosters creativity and innovation in web development.

  • Jhey on CodePen.io – We extend our heartfelt gratitude to Jhey for their innovative "Word Underline w/ CSS && clip-path" demo. This creative solution has significantly enhanced the visual appeal of our website, adding a dynamic and engaging underline effect to our text elements. Jhey's work showcases the power of CSS and clip-path in creating visually compelling user interfaces. You can explore more of Jhey's work on CodePen.io.
  • Baahubali on CodePen.io – Our testimonials section is brought to life thanks to Baahubali's excellent "Testimonial" demo. This contribution has allowed us to showcase our customer feedback in an attractive and user-friendly manner. The design's clean lines and intuitive layout have been integral in presenting our testimonials effectively. Check out Baahubali's other impressive works on CodePen.io.
  • Stefan C. on CodePen.io – We also acknowledge Stefan C. for the "Accordion Image Gallery" demo, which has been instrumental in creating an interactive and visually appealing gallery on our site. The accordion feature allows us to present our portfolio in a user-friendly and organized manner, enhancing the user experience significantly. Stefan C.'s creative approach to design has greatly enriched our site's functionality and aesthetics. Discover more of Stefan C.'s projects on CodePen.io.

Utilisation of FontAwesome

FontAwesome.com – Icons play a crucial role in enhancing the usability and visual appeal of our website. We utilize icons from FontAwesome throughout our site to ensure a clean, modern, and professional look. The comprehensive icon stylesheets provided by FontAwesome.com have been indispensable in creating a cohesive and user-friendly interface. These icons enhance navigation and improve the overall user experience by providing clear and intuitive visual cues. For more information, visit FontAwesome.com.

Utilisation of Flaticon Icons

Freepik – The use of Freepik's animated icons brings our homepage to life even further, we thank them for their work and encourage you to check out more of their work on flaticon.com
