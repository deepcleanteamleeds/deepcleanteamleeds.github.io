About Us

Meet The Deep Clean Team – Yorkshire's Local Cleaning Experts

The Deep Clean Team is one of Yorkshire's most trusted providers of professional exterior cleaning services. We're a dedicated team of jet washing and soft washing specialists who care about making every property shine, serving Leeds, Bradford and the wider Yorkshire area.

Our team serves both homeowners and businesses across Leeds, Bradford and the wider Yorkshire area. From patios and driveways to roof tiles and shop fronts, we bring powerful equipment, eco-conscious products and years of hands-on experience to every job. Whether you need a one-off clean or ongoing maintenance, we approach every job with the same level of care and commitment.

What We Do

Our tailored cleaning services include:

Jet Washing

High-pressure surface cleaning for patios, block paving, decking, and brickwork.

Chemical Washing

Stain removal using eco-safe agents - great for graffiti, tarmac, and rendered walls.

Soft Washing

Low-pressure cleaning for roofs, facades, and other sensitive areas.

Solar Panel Cleaning

Safe, streak-free cleaning that keeps solar panels working at full efficiency.
