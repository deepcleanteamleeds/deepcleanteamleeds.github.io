Our Cleaning Services Across Yorkshire

Based in Leeds and covering the whole of Yorkshire, including Bradford, The Deep Clean Team provides a wide range of residential and commercial exterior cleaning solutions. Jet Washing in Leeds, Bradford & Across Yorkshire

Powerful pressure washing for homes and businesses across Yorkshire. Jet washing is ideal for:

  • Driveways – Remove grime, stains, and algae to restore your property's kerb appeal.
  • Patios – Clean moss and dirt from slabs, stone, or tiles, leaving a fresh finish.
  • Shop Fronts – Maintain a clean business entrance to impress customers.
  • Beer Gardens – Provide guests a tidy, slip-free outdoor seating area.

Chemical Washing Services

Some surfaces benefit from a low-pressure chemical treatment using biodegradable products. Perfect for:

  • House Washing – Restore the exterior of homes including brick, render, and UPVC.
  • Event Venue Cleaning – Spotless preparation and post-event clean-ups.
  • Tarmac Restoration – Remove stubborn stains and brighten faded tarmac drives.

Soft Washing in Yorkshire

Gentle but effective soft washing suits delicate surfaces that require care without pressure damage:

  • Roof Cleaning – Remove moss and lichen while preserving tiles and felt.
  • House Siding – Ideal for wood, vinyl, or render facades.
  • Play Areas – Ensure a clean and safe play surface for children.
  • Fascia & Soffit Cleaning – Restore uPVC fascias and soffits to a bright, clean finish.
  • Conservatory Cleaning – Remove built-up grime from PVC and glass surfaces.

Solar Panel Cleaning

Dirty solar panels lose efficiency. Our safe, low-pressure solar panel cleaning service is ideal for:

  • Residential Solar Arrays – Restore panel performance and protect your investment.
  • Commercial & Solar Farm Cleaning – Large-scale cleaning to keep output at its peak.
  • Pigeon Proofing Support – Clear debris and mess that builds up under panels.

Specialist Cleaning Services

We also offer tailored cleaning for complex or commercial jobs:

  • Warehouse Cleaning – Large-scale indoor and exterior maintenance.
  • Insurance Work – Post-damage cleaning for floods, fire or vandalism.
  • Gutter Cleaning – Vacuum-assisted clearing for safe and efficient results.
  • Drain Jetting – Clear blocked drains and restore water flow fast.
