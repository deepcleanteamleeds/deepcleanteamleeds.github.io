Jet Washing

Our high-pressure jet washing services are designed to remove tough dirt, grime, algae, and moss from various surfaces. This powerful cleaning method is ideal for:



  • Driveways: Restore the appearance of your driveway, removing oil stains, tire marks, and built-up grime to enhance your home's curb appeal.

  • Shop Fronts: Keep your business entrance spotless, attracting more customers and creating a positive first impression.

  • Beer Gardens: Maintain a clean and hygienic outdoor seating area for your patrons, ensuring a pleasant experience.



Chemical Washing

For surfaces that require a gentler touch, our chemical washing services utilize eco-friendly cleaning agents to effectively remove stubborn stains without causing damage. This method is perfect for:



  • House Washing: Clean your home's exterior, including brick, siding, and stucco, to remove dirt, mold, and mildew, preserving its beauty and integrity.

  • Event Cleaning / Washing: Ensure your event space is spotless before and after your function, leaving no trace of dirt or spills.



Soft Washing

Our soft washing technique is ideal for delicate surfaces that cannot withstand the high pressure of jet washing. This method combines low-pressure water with specialized cleaning solutions to gently remove contaminants. Soft washing is suitable for:



  • Roofs: Remove moss, algae, and lichen without damaging your roof tiles, prolonging their lifespan and maintaining their appearance.

  • House Siding: Clean vinyl, wood, and other siding materials without risk of damage, ensuring your home looks its best.

  • Play Areas: Ensure children's play areas are free from mold, algae, and grime, providing a safe and clean environment for kids.
